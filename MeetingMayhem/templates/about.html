Scenario:

You are an agent who need to meet with ONE of your fellows at a secret time and place to exchange information. However, there is TWO other users besides you in the network and ONE of them is a malicious adversary trying to interrupt your guys meeting. You and another fellow will vote at the end of game session to decide a time and place to meet based on the conversation.


Goal:

  • Agree on the time and place with your fellow AND
  • The time and place need to be different from the adversary

Powerful Tools:

  • Symmetric Encryption: Encypting and decrypting your message with Shared Symmetric Key of you and the recipient.
  • Asymmetric Encryption: Encypting your message with Receiver's Public Key and decrypting the message with Reciver's own Private Key.
  • Signature: Signing the your message with Sender's (Your) own Private Key, and the receiver verifies your identity with Sender's (Your) Public Key.
